Things I Use

Last updated on July 3rd, 2024

Hardware

  • iPhone 6S (yes it’s old – no I won’t upgrade)
  • MacBook Air laptop
  • Kobo Clara 2E eReader
  • Fuji X-T4 digital camera

Software

  • Firefox browser with uBlock Origin
  • Ruby on Rails for web development
  • Obsidian for note taking. While not technically open source, Obsidian is ran by a small team and most of the code is free to view online. They make an amazing product that doesn’t lock you into an ecosystem. If they ever go out of business, I still have all of my notes locally on my machine as markdown files.
  • Todoist for task management. I’d like to find or make something open source and switch to that eventually
  • Calibre to de-DRM ebooks
  • iNaturalist for recording species I encounter in the field
  • Anki for remembering things
  • VSCode text editor
  • Signal for private messaging

Services

  • Vultr to host this site and create my own cloud
  • Porkbun for domain management
  • Obsidian Sync for securly syncing my notes on desktop and mobile
  • Waking Up for meditation
